About us

Badr Medical Group is a landmark healthcare provider in the eastern province of Saudi Arabia. Since its inception in 1998 as a dispensary with just a few specialists, Badr Medical Group has grown exponentially in Dammam, Qatif, Jubail and across Saudi Arabia through Remote Industrial Clinics. True to its founding principles, we have made premium healthcare accessible to both Saudi nationals and expatriates and its presence felt at every touch point of the medical value chain.
